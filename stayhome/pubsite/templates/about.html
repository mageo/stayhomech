{% blocktrans %}About us{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}StayHome.ch was founded by a Swiss citizen who in the situation of the COVID-19 epidemic, wanted to help his fellow citizens to easily find available delivery service close to their homes.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Later on, further volunteers joined the project, who share the same ambition to help the Swiss population as well as the larger and smaller business owners in Switzerland.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}To this day, StayHome.ch remains a completely volunteer and free initiative, funded entirely by its initiator, with the support of a few partners that offered professional services.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}We don’t display ads, we only collect the information from the companies that are willing to appear in the list and we are very careful with the way we handle these data. We don’t use them for marketing purpose, and we don’t sell them to third parties. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}To summarize, StayHome.ch is:{% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %}A directory of companies offering a home-delivery service in Switzerland.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}A free and volunteer-based initiative serving the Swiss population and economy.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}A promotion tool for a more local e-commerce that’s willing to support small businesses.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In addition to the companies we are listing directly, we also aggregate data from the external sources below.{% endblocktrans %}
